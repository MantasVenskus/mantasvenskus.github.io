Once-in-a-Lifetime Experience

We all know those famous online personalities, celebrated sportsmen, professional tutors, outspoken journalists, politicians, spiritual guides, presenters, life coaches and youtubers. We follow them on Twitter ,Youtube and Instagram, but never get a chance to meet them in real life in person. What we propose is that you spend real time face-to-face with your chosen expert as your personal tutor. Not online, but actually in person.

Let’s #Drill

Think Big, We’ll Do Our Best to Make It Happen

We challenge you to think big! Our aim is to truly inspire you to attain new levels of knowledge and skills in a professional, but sharing environment, with the professional of your choice. We ask to quote us the names of the professionals with whom you would enjoy sharing and acquiring knowledge, skills and friendship. It can be anyone or more than one person. We will take on the responsibility of sourcing your chosen tutor, and liaising between you and him/her to make it happen.

The Feeling is Mutual

In most cases, it’s not only you who wish to be in a classroom with the teacher of your dreams. Most professionals who have achieved their career goals develop a natural wish to reciprocate and share their knowledge and skills with others. It’s an innate quality within us, and has been for thousands of years. Therefore the classes are highly motivated from both sides as well as being great fun. And of course, you make friends and get to know your chosen professional in person, face to face.

Let’s #Drill

It's Easier When You Share the Burden

Some of the experts may cost thousands and more, which is a considerable amount for somebody to pay on their own; however thanks to crowdfunding, the cost is shared by a dozen or more people, as long as there are enough people to meet demand. As a result you can spend one-on–one time with your chosen tutor/professional in a real setting at a fraction of the cost.

Real in-Person Experience is Simply Irreplaceable

Professional and expert tuition. Real learning experience. Authentic results. This is the only way we believe it should be. Research has proven that people learn better when taught by real people in an engaging and friendly manner, rather than by virtual teachers or computers. We offer you the chance to spend time in an inspiring environment, learning, making friends and getting to improve your knowledge and life-skills with entertaining professionals and experts, working together and having fun.
